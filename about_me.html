    About Me

    My name is Fabrizio Casati, I'm an Italian computer engineer student currently working on his master thesis while also spending a period abroad in Klagenfurt (Austria) working as a research assistant at the Institute of Networked and Embedded Systems (NES).

    As a computer engineer, I'm mainly interested in topics related to artificial intelligence and machine learning, such as robotics and reinforcement learning: my master thesis covers in fact the implementation in ROS of some theoretical models for the exploration of an unknown environment using a multi-robot system with energy constraints (i.e., robots have a limited battery life).
